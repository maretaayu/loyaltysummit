About

Welcome to the first and only Loyalty Summit in Indonesia, the premier event for Marketing Directors, Trade Marketers, Sales Directors, CEOs, in Indonesia.

This one-day summit brings together over 500 professionals from diverse industries such as FMCG, Retail & Fashion, Building Material Brands, Automotive Spare Part Brands, Pharmaceutical, and more. Our summit provides a platform for industry leaders, experts, and practitioners to share their knowledge, experiences, and strategies for driving business growth through effective loyalty and rewards programs.
