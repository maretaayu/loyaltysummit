About Conference

Welcome to the first Loyalty & Rewards Conference, the premier event for Channel Marketers, Trade Marketers, Marketing Directors and Sales Directors in Indonesia. This one-day conference brings together over 500 professionals from diverse industries.

Our conference provides a platform for industry leaders, experts, and practitioners to share their knowledge, experiences, and strategies for driving business growth through effective loyalty and rewards programs.
